BMI-Calculator

The BMI Calculator is a simple yet powerful application developed in C# that allows users to
calculate their Body Mass Index (BMI) quickly and accurately. BMI is a widely used indicator of
body fatness and is calculated based on a person's weight and height. With the BMI Calculator,
users can assess their current health status, set realistic fitness goals, and monitor their progress
towards achieving a healthy weight.
